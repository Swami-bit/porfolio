Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering

Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering (SSGMCE) is a premier engineering institution located in Shegaon, Maharashtra. Established in 1983, it is affiliated with Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University and approved by AICTE.

The college offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering disciplines and is known for its excellent academic standards, infrastructure, and placement opportunities.

SSGMCE is committed to providing quality technical education and producing competent engineers who can contribute to the technological advancement of the nation.
